What SlouchX Does

SlouchX intelligently monitors your posture and provides real-time feedback to help you maintain proper alignment throughout your day.

1.

Easy Placement & Setup

Place SlouchX on your back using the adhesive, and set up your goals using SlouchX's mobile App. The device attaches discreetly and comfortably to your upper back, becoming virtually invisible under clothing.

2.

Real-Time Posture Feedback

Get real-time posture feedback, for instant posture correction, and track your progress via the mobile app. When slouching is detected, SlouchX gently vibrates to remind you to correct your position.

3.

Personalized Training Program

SlouchX adapts to your unique body mechanics and gradually builds your core and back strength through a personalized training program. The device adjusts its sensitivity over time, helping you maintain proper posture longer.

4.

Comprehensive Analytics

Track your progress over time with detailed insights into your posture habits. View your improvement trends, session histories, and receive personalized recommendations to further enhance your posture.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about SlouchX.

How does SlouchX attach to my body?
SlouchX attaches to your upper back using a medical-grade, hypoallergenic adhesive that's gentle on the skin. The adhesive is designed for all-day wear and can be easily removed without causing irritation. Each adhesive can be used for 3-5 days depending on your activity level.
How long does the battery last?
SlouchX's battery provides up to 30 hours of continuous use on a single charge. For most users, this means 3-4 days of regular use. The device comes with a magnetic charging dock that fully recharges the device in just 90 minutes.
Is SlouchX water-resistant?
Yes, SlouchX has an IP67 rating, making it dust-tight and able to withstand immersion in water up to 1 meter for 30 minutes. This means you can wear it during workouts and not worry about sweat or light rain, though we don't recommend swimming with it.
How does the training program work?
SlouchX's progressive training program adapts to your posture improvement over time. When you first start using SlouchX, it gives you more time to correct your posture before alerting you. As your posture improves, the device gradually becomes more sensitive, helping you maintain perfect posture for longer periods. This gradual approach helps build the supporting muscles needed for good posture without causing fatigue.
Can I customize the sensitivity of the device?
Absolutely! Through the SlouchX app, you can customize the sensitivity of the device, the type and intensity of the vibration alerts, and even set "focus periods" where the device becomes less sensitive during important meetings or events. The app gives you complete control over your posture training experience.
